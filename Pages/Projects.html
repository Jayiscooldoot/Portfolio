My Projects

In this memory game colors will appear on the screen then disappear after an amount of time. They will then reappear after some more time has passed, when they coomeback one of the colors will be different from those of the previous set. Your task is to spot the one that is different and click on it. As you progress the game will get more difficult by adding more colors, and giving you less and less time to see the colors. Good Luck!
